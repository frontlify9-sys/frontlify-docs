# Welcome to Frontlify Documentation
Frontlify is a powerful customer feedback and engagement platform designed to help businesses collect reviews, improve customer satisfaction, and grow faster.
## What You Can Do With Frontlify
- Collect instant feedback using QR-based forms  
- Get real-time reports and analytics  
- Manage customer sentiment  
- Improve service quality with data-driven insights  
## Why Choose Frontlify?
Frontlify helps businesses turn customer insights into meaningful improvements.  
It is easy to use, fast to deploy, and perfect for restaurants, salons, retail stores, gyms, and more.
